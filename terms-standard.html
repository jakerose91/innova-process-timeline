05Replacement Guarantee

Replacement Guarantee

If a Candidate placed by Innova leaves their employment within 13 weeks of commencement (inclusive of any notice period), Innova will use reasonable efforts to introduce a replacement Candidate for the same role at no additional fee, subject to the following conditions:

  • The Candidate’s departure is voluntary and not due to redundancy, organisational restructure, material change to the role, or termination by the Client (including performance-related termination);
  • The replacement search is limited to the original role scope, including substantially similar responsibilities, seniority, and remuneration;
  • The Client continues to engage Innova on an exclusive basis for the replacement search; and
  • All invoices have been paid in full in accordance with agreed terms.

No refunds will be provided under any circumstances. Replacement availability is not guaranteed.
