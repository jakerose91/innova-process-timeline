OUR PROCESS

Seven stages. Brief-first. Disciplined throughout.

8–10 weeks engagement to offer
10–15 candidates longlisted
4–8 candidates shortlisted
01
Scoping & brief
Week 1

Stakeholder conversations, written brief, operating model agreed before market.

02
Market mapping
Weeks 1–2

400–600 profiles reviewed. Assessed on context and trajectory.

03
Calibration
Weeks 1–2

We test the brief against the market and recalibrate before the shortlist.

04
Shortlist
Weeks 3–4

Four to eight candidates, written executive profiles, a clear recommendation.

05
Interview cadence
Weeks 4–8

Weekly cadence calls. Pipeline status, candidate feedback, market observations.

06
Offer & close
Weeks 8–10

Benchmarking, structuring, and the conversations that land the offer.

07
Transition
Post-placement

Counteroffers, nerves, and the first months in seat. We stay involved.

Indicative timeline: 8–10 weeks from engagement to accepted offer. When the brief is urgent, we compress without sacrificing quality.
