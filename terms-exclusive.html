Exclusive engagement — Terms of Business

Standard terms applying to exclusive search engagements with Innova Search Pty Ltd. Per-deal commercials (engagement fee, completion fee, exclusivity period) are set out in the proposal accepted alongside these terms.

01Engagement

The engagement

These terms apply where the Client (the engaging counterparty named in the proposal) appoints Innova Search Pty Ltd (Innova) on an exclusive basis to identify and introduce a candidate for a senior leadership role. Exclusivity allows Innova to represent the Client properly in the market and gives the search the depth of attention it requires.

The role, exclusivity period, and engagement amounts are set out in the proposal accepted alongside these terms.

02Fee

Placement fee

Placement fees are calculated as a percentage of the candidate's Total Annual Gross Remuneration (TAGR), defined as base salary plus superannuation plus any sign-on or guaranteed bonuses.

TAGR (AUD)Fee
$1 – $149,99920%
$150,000 – $219,99922%
$220,000 – $349,99924%
$350,000 and above26%

All fees are exclusive of GST and payable within 30 days of invoice. If GST becomes payable under applicable law, it will be added to the invoice. Any dispute regarding an invoice must be raised in writing within seven (7) days of the invoice date.

03Payment

Payment schedule

For exclusive engagements, the placement fee is split across two milestones, with the specific amounts set out in the engaging proposal.

MilestoneTiming
Engagement feePayable on signing of the engaging proposal.
Completion paymentPayable on the candidate's commencement date.
04Exclusivity

Exclusivity

The engagement is exclusive for the period set out in the engaging proposal, from the date of signing. During this period the Client agrees not to engage any other search firm or recruitment agency for the same role.

05Guarantee

Replacement guarantee

If a candidate placed by Innova leaves their employment within 13 weeks of commencement (inclusive of any notice period), Innova will use reasonable efforts to introduce a replacement candidate for the same role at no additional fee, subject to the following conditions:

  • The candidate's departure is voluntary and not due to redundancy, organisational restructure, material change to the role, or termination by the Client (including performance-related termination).
  • The replacement search is limited to the original role scope, including substantially similar responsibilities, seniority, and remuneration.
  • The Client continues to engage Innova on an exclusive basis for the replacement search.
  • All invoices have been paid in full in accordance with agreed terms.

No refunds will be provided under any circumstances. Replacement availability is not guaranteed.

06Expenses

Expenses

Direct search expenses are charged at cost with prior written approval from the Client. No mark-ups are applied.

07Confidentiality

Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep the engagement, candidate identities, and any commercially sensitive information shared during the search confidential. This obligation survives termination of this agreement.

08Liability

Liability

Innova's liability under this agreement is capped at the fees paid under it.

09Law

Governing law
